Where's Hank

You can find Hank hosting a number of different YouTube channels and Podcasts

vlogbrothers >

Vlogbrothers started in 2007 with Hank and his brother John making videos to each other instead of talking. That project has been the basis of basically everything else on this website, largely because of the support of the amazing community that grew up around the videos.

CrashCourse >

Crash Course is an educational YouTube channel that is used in nearly every school in the US, and by teachers, students all over the world in subjects ranging from chemistry to world history to business.

SciShow >

Seven days a week, SciShow delves into science news, history, concepts, and revelations! With over a billion views, it’s one of the most popular science shows in the world.

SciShow Psych >

Hank and Brit Garner host “SciShow Psych” a twice weekly dive into the human mind and how it understands itself and the rest of the world.

SciShow Space >

Twice a week “SciShow: Space” covers the complexities of space exploration, astrophysics, and astronomy.

hankschannel >

Hankschannel is a place for Hank to put thoughts and projects and just to mess around on YouTube without too many people watching!

SciShow Tangents >

A lightly competitive knowledge showcase! Four friends challenge each other to out-fact each other’s amazing science facts.

Dear Hank and John >

Dear Hank and John is a podcast where two brothers answer questions, give dubious advice, and bring you all the week’s news from both Mars (the planet) and AFC Wimbledon (the third tier English soccer team.)
